About Al Omiara Group

Shihab M M
Director

Welcome to the Al Omaira Group web site. Thank you for having taken the time.With 27 years of experience we have been committed to provide our customers a wide range of products of a high quality and competitive price in order to meet customer full satisification . Al Omaira is the one of main distributers of olives, cheese, pickels, jams, olive oils, honey, ghee, halawa etc in Uae and Oman. Al Omaira was established in 1991 as a food stuff suppliers, today the company is the one of the leading distributers in united arab emirates. HACCP certifications are indications of our commitment to delivering a better quality product. Al Omaira logistics works 24/7 to fulfill customer requirements. We look to build a win-win relationship with our customers. Customer service is a top priority for our company. Ensuring best-in-class customer service levels that meet the requirements of customers. Our strength is our staff and customers. The sales operations are organized in groups of products, each having its own dedicated sales team to deliver the right focus by product and by customer. These groups cover directly all channels including modern trade, wholesale, traditional, horeca and specialty channels.
